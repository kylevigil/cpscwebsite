Cal Poly Men's Soccer Club

The official page of the Cal Poly men's club soocer team. This page contains information about schedule, roster, contact, tryouts, and more.

Find Out More

We're all best friends!

Cal Poly men's club soccer is the perfect blend of academics and highly competitive soccer. We are a close knit group that have become brothers both on and off the pitch.

The team has a fall and spring season as well as a handful of tournaments throughout the year. In season we play schools up and down the coast and in tournaments, especially nationals, we play teams across the country. We have games on the weekends and early morning (6:15 am) practices. This allows us to work around the college schedules to ensure that school comes first. Even though we put a large emphasis on academics, this is still a highly competive team and we have won almost every competition available to us.

Tryout Information

We have one tryout before the fall and spring seasons. The fall tryout is the weekend before the week of welcome because our school starts so late, we have to start early to compete with the other schools in the area. Check the calendar for the tryout times and locations. Tryouts have a $15 fee.
